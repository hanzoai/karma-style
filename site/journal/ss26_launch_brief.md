# Signature — Launch Brief

## The idea
Two suits, one point of view. **Signature** is the smallest possible collection —
a strappy velvet set and a clean scarlet maillot — chosen because they argue the
same thing from opposite ends: that swimwear can be *dressed*. Valentina brings
weight and hardware; the Scarlet One-Piece brings restraint and a single colour.
Together they set the house tone: monochrome studio, real materials, made to
photograph.

## The pieces
- **Valentina** — deep-wine crushed velvet, gold open-heart hardware, criss-cross
  hip straps, full thigh-wrap garter. Adjustable halter, high-leg. Our
  most-requested set. **$158.**
- **Scarlet One-Piece** — matte scarlet lycra, sculpted open back, seamless.
  Sun-ready and made to photograph. **$168.**

## Voice
Monochrome. Editorial. Sensual, never cheesy. Lowercase on social, restraint on
the journal. We name the material and the cut and let the light do the rest. No
"elevate your look," no exclamation stacks, no filler.

## Channels & sequence
1. **Journal (blog)** — two editorial posts, *Valentina, in Velvet* and *The Case
   for One Colour*. The anchor content; everything else points here.
2. **Instagram feed** — one hero per suit (velvet lifestyle; scarlet ecom-front),
   captions lowercase, 6 hashtags, CTA `→ karma.style`.
3. **TikTok / Reels** — the velvet "light like water" cut; the scarlet "wait for
   the turn" open-back reveal.
4. **Campaign still** — the 1:1 square as the paid/feed unit.
