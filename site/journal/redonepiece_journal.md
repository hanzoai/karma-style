# The Case for One Colour

Red is a decision. You cannot wear it quietly, so we stopped trying.

The Scarlet One-Piece is a single, uninterrupted field of matte lycra — no gloss,
no print, no seam allowed to break the line. Matte matters here: a shine would
turn the colour loud, and we wanted it deep instead, the kind of red that holds
its shape in full sun and still looks composed at dusk.

The work is all in the back. A sculpted open scoop drops low and clean, cut so the
suit stays put while it opens up — the front reads as classic maillot, the turn
reads as something else entirely. Seamless construction keeps the surface honest.
Nothing to adjust, nothing to hide.

Sun-ready, and made to photograph. One colour, said properly.
